# Product Images

Place your product images in this folder with these filenames:

- `iphone15.png` or `iphone15.jpg` - iPhone 15 product image
- `samsung_galaxy.png` or `samsung_galaxy.jpg` - Samsung Galaxy product image  
- `google_pixel.png` or `google_pixel.jpg` - Google Pixel product image

## Recommended Image Specifications
- Format: PNG or JPG
- Size: 800x1200 pixels (2:3 ratio works well for product cards)
- File size: Keep under 500KB for optimal performance

## Note
Until you add actual images, the app will show placeholder colored containers.
